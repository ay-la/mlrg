Hi all!

The first meeting of the semester will be Friday, September 2nd, and we need to agree on a meeting time. Please go to the Doodle poll and fill in the form.

  • Game Theory (continued)
  • Linear Algebra (Vector Space Semantics, Support Vector Machines, Matlab)
  • Chaos Theory
  • Network Theory
  • Fourier Transforms, EEG Math
  • Model Theoretic Syntax
  • Categorial Grammar and Type-Logical Grammar
